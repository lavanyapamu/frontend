2. Platform Description

ArtFlare is an online marketplace that connects artists with art buyers, facilitating the sale and purchase of original artwork, prints, digital art, sculptures, handicrafts, and photography.

3. User Accounts and Registration

To use certain features of ArtFlare, you must register for an account. You agree to:

  • Provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration
  • Maintain and update your information to keep it accurate
  • Maintain the security of your password and account
  • Accept responsibility for all activities under your account

4. Artist Responsibilities

Artists using ArtFlare agree to:

  • Ensure all artwork listings are original or properly licensed
  • Provide accurate descriptions, dimensions, and high-quality images
  • Fulfill orders within specified timeframes (typically 1-7 business days)
  • Package artwork securely to prevent damage during shipping
  • Maintain professional communication with buyers
  • Comply with all applicable laws regarding art sales and shipping

5. Buyer Responsibilities

Buyers using ArtFlare agree to:

  • Provide accurate shipping and payment information
  • Respect artists' intellectual property rights
  • Communicate professionally and respectfully with artists
  • Review artwork descriptions and policies before purchasing
  • Report any issues within the specified timeframe

6. Payments and Fees

ArtFlare charges a service fee of 8% on successful transactions plus payment processing fees. Payment processing is handled through secure third-party providers including Stripe and PayPal.

7. Intellectual Property Rights

Artists retain ownership of their intellectual property. Buyers receive specific usage rights as outlined in individual artwork listings. Unauthorized reproduction or commercial use is prohibited.

8. Dispute Resolution

ArtFlare provides mediation services for disputes between artists and buyers. All parties agree to attempt resolution through our platform before pursuing external legal action.

9. Limitation of Liability

ArtFlare's liability is limited to the transaction value. We are not responsible for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

Shipping Policy

Last updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

1. Shipping Responsibility

Individual artists are responsible for packaging and shipping their artwork to buyers. ArtFlare facilitates the transaction but does not handle physical shipping or logistics.

2. Processing Time

  • Ready-to-ship items: 1-3 business days
  • Custom artwork: As specified in the listing (typically 7-21 days)
  • Large sculptures: May require additional processing time

3. Shipping Methods and Timeframes

Method Domestic Delivery International Delivery Tracking
Standard Shipping 5-10 business days 10-21 business days Basic tracking
Express Shipping 2-5 business days 5-10 business days Full tracking
Premium Shipping 1-3 business days 3-7 business days Real-time tracking

4. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are determined by the artist based on:

  • Artwork size, weight, and fragility
  • Destination location
  • Selected shipping method
  • Insurance requirements

All shipping costs are clearly displayed before purchase completion.

5. International Shipping

International orders may be subject to:

  • Customs duties and import taxes (buyer responsibility)
  • Extended delivery times due to customs processing
  • Additional documentation requirements
  • Restrictions on certain art materials or sizes

6. Packaging Standards

Artists are required to:

  • Use appropriate protective materials for artwork type
  • Include adequate cushioning and moisture protection
  • Use sturdy boxes or tubes for safe transport
  • Include handling instructions for fragile items

7. Tracking and Updates

Tracking information is provided when available. For questions about your shipment, contact the artist directly through our messaging system or ArtFlare support.

Return Policy

Last updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

1. Return Eligibility

Returns are accepted under the following conditions:

  • Item arrives damaged during shipping
  • Item is significantly different from listing description
  • Item has manufacturing defects
  • Return request is made within 14 days of delivery

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom commissioned artwork (unless it doesn't meet agreed specifications)
  • Digital downloads and prints
  • Items damaged by buyer negligence
  • Items returned after 14-day window

3. Return Process

1
Contact Artist or Support

Within 14 days of delivery, contact the artist or ArtFlare support

2
Provide Documentation

Submit photos and detailed description of the issue

3
Await Authorization

Wait for return authorization and shipping instructions

4
Ship Item Back

Package securely and ship to provided address with tracking

4. Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the artist receives and approves the return. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

5. Return Shipping Costs

  • Artist Error: ArtFlare covers return shipping
  • Damaged in Transit: Shipping carrier covers costs
  • Buyer Preference: Buyer covers return shipping

6. Dispute Resolution

If you cannot resolve a return issue directly with the artist, contact ArtFlare support for mediation assistance. We're committed to fair resolution for all parties.
